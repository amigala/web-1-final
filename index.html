Sticker Project:

This is a sticker design that I created for my Visual Communication 1 design class. I created a plastic water bottle and a dead sea turtle to be the logo of the bottle to spread the message of the plastic pollution that is currently occuring in our society.

Packaging Project:

This is a packaging project that I designed for my Visual Communication 1 design class. We were giving the design challenge to take two different companies and combine them into a package, so I decided to do McDonald's happy meal packaging and changed it to a Whole Foods happy meal.
